Controls

Light intensity 60
The turtles seek light. Reduce to zero for aimless wandering.
Obstacles — drag to move, drag end handle to rotate. Mirror creates a feedback loop — drag the frozen turtle to relocate and release.
Trail colour encodes state: yellow = seeking · orange = hesitating · red = avoiding · cyan = frozen.
Touch — tap a turtle to inject a stimulus. Drag when frozen to relocate.
